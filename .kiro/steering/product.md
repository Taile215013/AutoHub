# AutoHub – Product Overview

AutoHub is a Vietnamese-market automotive e-commerce and service platform. It serves customers who want to browse, purchase, or book services for vehicles and spare parts.

## Core Capabilities

- **Vehicle catalog** – Browse, filter, and purchase cars and motorcycles
- **Spare parts store** – Search and buy spare parts with vehicle compatibility filtering
- **Service booking** – Book automotive services (placeholder, in development)
- **Shopping cart & orders** – Session-based cart, order tracking
- **User accounts** – Registration, login, profile management, order history
- **Admin panel** – Manage vehicles, spare parts, brands, services, and view dashboard stats

## Target Users

- **Customers** – Browse inventory, manage cart, place orders, book services
- **Admins** – Manage product catalog, inventory, and business metrics

## Business Context

- Primary market is Vietnam; UI text and error messages are in Vietnamese
- Currency and pricing are decimal-based (VND implied)
- User rank system: Bronze / Silver / Gold tiers
- Authentication is custom session-based (no ASP.NET Identity)
